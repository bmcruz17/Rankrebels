Where it stands

Great milk. But the money comes after the work.

The herd needs feeding every single day — that cost never pauses. Yet sales run through Facebook comments and DMs, pickups get paid (or not) in person, and new members can only find you if they already know your name. The result: cash shows up after you've already fed the cows, and plenty of demand never finds you at all.

▲ The squeeze today

Every pickup, 10–20 people line up to pay one at a time — cash, Venmo, or Zelle. Orders live in DMs, there are no-shows and "I'll get you next time," and nobody new finds you on Google.

◆ What changes

Members join and prepay online, a real website ranks for the searches people already type, and pickup becomes hand-off only. Predictable cash, in advance — the pastures stay full.
