01 · The field + corporate app

iPhone & Android, one team

Field reps, crews, subcontractors, and corporate officers in a single app — so the job in the field and the job in the office are finally the same job.

📍
GPS-stamped captures & redlines

Daily reports, photos, and as-built redlines tied to the GIS design — online or offline in the field, synced the moment there's signal.

🧭
Dispatch & work orders

Assign crews and subs, push the route and scope to their phone, and watch status move from assigned → in progress → QA → done.

⏱️
Timesheets & production

Crews log hours and units in the field; payroll and billing read the same numbers — no double entry.

🔗
Design → field → invoice

The model the best fiber contractors are moving to: one thread from the GIS plan to the as-built to the invoice. No re-keying between five systems.
