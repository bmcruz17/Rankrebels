Where the days go

You run two businesses at once — and hold both in your head.

You're the agent and the property manager. Listings, showings, and offers on one side; rent, repairs, owners, and vendors on the other. It works because you never drop a ball. That's exhausting — and it doesn't have to be manual.

◆ Today, without a system

  • Listings live on a portal you don't control — no site that's yours.
  • Rent due dates and who paid live in your memory and your texts.
  • Vendor invoices and "when is that inspection?" scattered across email.
  • Owners call to ask "how's my property doing?" — you rebuild the answer each time.
  • Income and expenses aren't totaled until tax season panic.

◆ With Realty by the Sea, the platform

  • A coastal website that is your brand — list a home in two minutes.
  • Every rent due date and payment tracked, color-coded, never missed.
  • Vendor due dates and invoices in one calendar with reminders.
  • An owner a tap away from a live, beautiful report on their property.
  • Income vs. expenses totaled live — you always know where you stand.

The full vision

Everything you carry, in one place that works for you.

The preview above is the front door. Behind it is a private backend built around how a real estate agent and property manager actually spends her day.

Your own website

A coastal, mobile-first site that's yours — listings, your story, and lead capture that sends buyers and renters to you, not a portal.

List & manage properties

Add or edit a listing in two minutes. Sold, rented, off-market — one tap. It updates your site everywhere, instantly.

Rent & income tracking

Every unit, every tenant, every due date — color-coded paid / due / late. You always know who owes what, and what's landed.

Expenses & vendors

Log a repair, tie it to a property, track vendor due dates. Income minus expenses, live — no shoebox of receipts in April.

Inbox, read for you

Calendar & due dates

Showings, inspections, rent dates, vendor deadlines — one calendar with reminders so nothing is ever a surprise.

Owner portals

Each owner gets a private, read-only view of just their property — rent, expenses, payouts — so they stop calling to ask.

Private & secure

Your data is yours alone, locked to your login. Owners see only theirs. Bank-grade auth, daily backups.

Tax-ready, always

Every dollar in and out is categorized as it happens. When tax time comes, it's a one-click export — not a weekend.
