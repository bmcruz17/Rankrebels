Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RankRebels.AI LLC ("RankRebels," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit rankrebels.ai (the "Site") or contact us. By using the Site, you agree to this Policy.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, business name, email address, phone number (optional), and any message you provide.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we collect basic usage data through analytics — for example pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser type, and referring links — via cookies and similar technologies.

Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it. Analytics cookies are only set after you accept via our cookie banner; if you decline, analytics cookies are not stored. You can also clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time, and opt out of Google Analytics using Google's opt-out add-on.

Bot protection (Cloudflare Turnstile)

To protect our website chat and forms from spam and automated abuse, we use Cloudflare Turnstile. Turnstile may collect and process information about your device and browser (such as browser characteristics, IP address, and interaction signals) to confirm you are a human and not a bot. This runs in the background and does not use tracking cookies to follow you across sites. This processing is carried out by Cloudflare; see the Cloudflare Turnstile Privacy Policy and Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide a website/SEO audit or proposal.

We do not sell your personal information.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.
